## 🤖 REZ MIND CAPABILITIES

### 1. NATURAL LANGUAGE CHAT

```
Customer: "I want to order something spicy but vegetarian"
REZ:      "Great choice! Based on your preferences, I'd recommend:
           • Paneer Tikka Masala - ₹280
           • Veg Manchurian - ₹220
           • Spicy Schezwan Fried Rice - ₹180
           
           All are vegetarian and moderately spicy.
           Would you like to add any of these to your order?"

Customer: "Is the Paneer fresh today?"
REZ:      "Yes! Our Paneer Tikka is made fresh daily with farmhouse 
           paneer. It's been one of our top-selling dishes this week.
           Would you like me to add it to your order?"
```

### 2. ORDER MANAGEMENT

```
Customer: "I want to book a table for 4 at 8pm tonight"
REZ:      "Let me check availability...
           
           Great news! We have a table for 4 available at 8pm.
           Shall I book it under your name?
           
           Name: [Customer Name from profile]
           Phone: [Phone from profile]
           Party size: 4
           Time: 8:00 PM
           
           Confirm booking? (Yes/No)"

Customer: "Yes"
REZ:      "✅ Booking confirmed!
           Table for 4 at 8:00 PM tonight.
           See you then!"
```

### 3. BOOKING & RESERVATIONS

```
Hotel Room Service:
Customer: "I'd like to book a massage for tomorrow at 3pm"
REZ:      "Our spa offers:
           • Swedish Massage - 60 min - ₹2,500
           • Deep Tissue - 60 min - ₹3,000
           • Aromatherapy - 90 min - ₹3,500
           
           Which would you prefer?"
           
Customer: "The Swedish one"
REZ:      "Perfect! Booking spa appointment...
           
           📅 Swedish Massage
           📍 Hotel Spa, Floor 3
           🕐 Tomorrow, 3:00 PM
           💰 ₹2,500 (charged to room)
           
           You'll receive a confirmation shortly.
           See you there!"
```

### 4. ENQUIRIES & INFORMATION

```
Customer: "What's the hotel's checkout time?"
REZ:      "Standard checkout is 11:00 AM.
           
           Would you like me to:
           • Check your current checkout time (based on your booking)
           • Request a late checkout (subject to availability)
           • Extend checkout to a specific time?"

Customer: "Can I have late checkout until 2pm?"
REZ:      "I'll check with the front desk...
           
           Late checkout until 2:00 PM has been approved!
           Your new checkout time is now 2:00 PM.
           
           Is there anything else I can help you with?"
```
